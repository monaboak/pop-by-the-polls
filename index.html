Pop by the Polls

This is a short research article for the 2020 election. It covers four issues currently being discussed, a candidate running for President of the United States, a polling place, and a sample ballot.

Issues

These are the issues that voters and candidates are discussing this general election.

COVID-19

The current U.S. administration's response to the COVID-19 pandemic has been under a great deal of scrutiny. One major issue is how both presidential candidates will establish a plan to rehabilite the country in order to survive and overcome COVID.

Taxes

Tax cuts and raises are another issue being discussed as the general election is on the horizon. One proposal is to raise taxes for the wealthy (those who make over $400,000 a year. While another proposal is to actually cut taxes for the wealthy and middle class.

Economy

Ever since COVID-19 hit the U.S. and caused an econmic shut down. There has been the issue of exactly how the next president will rebuild the economy. America has seen a soaring unemployment rate, thousands of small business closures, and a stock market crash.

Supreme Court

With the recent passing of Supreme Court Justice member Ruth Bader Ginsburg, there is yet another vacancy on the Supreme Court. There is popular debate on exactly who should fill the vacancy and why.
